Main Navigation Strategy

Our site is designed to ensure users can navigate with ease. The main navigation bar is positioned at the top of every page, providing access to all primary sections of the website. Here's an overview of the navigation strategy:

  • Top Navigation Bar: Includes links to home, product information, pricing, and customer support areas.
  • Sidebar: Used for secondary navigation links, such as account settings and additional resources.
  • Breadcrumbs: Displayed at the top of content pages, allowing users to trace their path back to the homepage.
  • Footer Navigation: Contains links to legal information, contact pages, and social media accounts.
